About Me

I’m a sound editor with over four years of experience delivering broadcast-quality audio for network television and documentaries. My specialty lies in dialogue editing and sound effects for unscripted and factual TV, as well as sound design for documentary storytelling. I’m Pro Tools certified, skilled in Logic, Ableton, and iZotope RX, and thrive in fast-paced, collaborative post-production environments.
